Destination Imagination

Team Details

  • Team Name: Infinite Creative Crew (ICC)
  • Teammates: Tarj Mecwan, Satya Bandaragoda, Dan Joshwa,Shaelyn Gerradette, Natalie Giovi, and Dafny Joshwa
  • Place: 2nd place

There were 81 teams competing in Show and Tech, Middle Level Challenge. Infinite Creative Crew Won 2nd place overall. Destination Imagination (DI) is a global educational nonprofit dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators, leaders and creative problem solvers. We programmed and created a rotating stage where we were able to act out a performance. We used python and javascript to make voice activation and camera signals possible. We took apart of metal scraps, wood planks, and tin to create a movable stage. For the creative aspect, we performed a story where we travelled to a nearby planet using a rocket ship (we created a "ship" that went into the air (2 meters)) and we found a group of aliens who performed a song. We went through 3 tournaments:"Regional","State", and "Global".
