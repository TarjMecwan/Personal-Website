Paint Project | Theme: Stranger Things

Information

  • Year: 11
  • Grade:: 100%
  • Theme: Stranger Things

For our school, we had a mid-term project to desgin a simple paint program like windows Paint but with a little more style. The program is suppose to allow the user to use the mouse to draw simple pictures and to save them onto a file. We were also encouraged to make our own creative design for the layout for our screen. The features I add were: load and save, undo and redo, pencil, eraser, spray paint, color picker, brush, highlighter, fill paint, fill and unfilled rectangle and ellipse, polygon maker, and line maker. I also included 6 different stickers and backgrounds. I've also included a spotify themed music playlist into the application where you can "scrub" through the songs.
